A Digital Sanctuary

Unlearning the World.
Reclaiming the Human Spirit.

MyBibleLens is an AI-powered spiritual research platform built to help you understand yourself, deepen your faith, and leave a legacy rooted in God.

The Mission

My purpose in making this app was to guide me through challenges in life and really hone me into understanding what God has in store for me, and understanding how much Jesus Christ loves me and how He's by my side no matter what I'm going through.

For me to understand, I need more than just Christ in the Bible. I need something to help me understand — that's the Wisdom Guide. I want to understand my dreams — that's Dreams. The root meaning of things around me — Etymology. The chemicals in our bodies — Neuroscience. How to speak wisdom to others — Philosopher. And when I get distracted, lost, or forget who I am — The Refiner.

How can I pray stronger, manifest deeper? The Orb.

Where can I put all of this? Study Repository. How can I make connections? Infinite Canvas. How can I write it down? Reflection. How can I see what my people are studying? Beloved.

Founder's Note

Denzel built MyBibleLens out of a necessity to "Unlearn the World." Frustrated by the lack of depth in modern education, he taught himself the logic and architecture of AI to build a sanctuary for the human spirit. He is the visionary behind the Logos Guide and the curator of the Pure Existence Exchange.

Your Purpose with the App

The purpose for you is to understand yourself, to get away from the chaos of social media and the people around you, to understand God stronger, and to go to church understanding what they're talking about. To go to Bible studies and show people what you drew in your infinite canvas, having fun with it, already being in your Bible, writing things down physically — because it's all here, all here for you.

This isn't just an app. This is something you can one day sit down and share with your children during Bible study. You can point to a date, a season, a year of your life, and say, "This is what God brought me through. This is what He showed me. This is how everything is connected."

"You can finally give your children something deeper than information — you can give them legacy."

I want families to be families again. I want communities to be communities again. I don't want outside voices teaching our children fear, shame, confusion, unworthiness, depression, anxiety, or leading them deeper into sin. I want them to know the truth. I want them to know love. I want them to know God.

At the end of the day, this is bigger than tech. Bigger than design. Bigger than me. This is just me putting God first.
