70,000 Seats.
One Shared Mission.

The number isn't random. Jesus sent seventy disciples ahead of Him into every town He was about to enter (Luke 10:1). The Table of Nations names seventy peoples descended from Noah — the original family of the earth (Genesis 10). Seventy is the number of commission, of family, of the world being reached.

We multiplied that by 1,000 — the number of generations God promises His covenant to those who love Him (Deuteronomy 7:9). Seventy disciples for the next thousand generations. That's 70,000 Founding Members. Not investors. Not customers. Sent ones.

  • And much, much more…
